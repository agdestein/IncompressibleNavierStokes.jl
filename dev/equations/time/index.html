Time discretization

The spatially discretized Navier-Stokes equations form a differential-algebraic system, with an ODE for the velocity

\[\Omega \frac{\mathrm{d} V_h}{\mathrm{d} t} = F(V_h) - (G p_h + y_G)\]

and an algebraic equation for the pressure

\[A p_h = g.\]
